Finishes

Surface options for granite.

Every finish below is available on request — tell us the application and we'll suggest the right one.

Polished

High gloss — the standard finish for worktops and floors.

Honed

Matte surface, popular for kitchen worktops with a softer look.

Flamed

Rough, heat-treated surface for exterior paving and steps.

Bush-Hammered

Textured, slip-resistant surface for outdoor cladding.
