Finishes

Surface options for travertine.

Every finish below is available on request — tell us the application and we'll suggest the right one.

Honed & Filled

Smooth, closed surface — the standard interior finish.

Tumbled

Rounded edges, aged look — popular for courtyards and pool decks.

Brushed

Textured surface that highlights the stone's natural pores.

Polished

Glossy surface for interior floors and feature walls.

Colour Variants

Available travertine tones.

Natural stone varies block to block — these represent typical colour ranges, not an exact match guarantee.

Classico

Warm beige, the most widely used travertine tone.

Silver

Cool grey-beige with a contemporary feel.

Noce

Rich walnut brown, often used as an accent stone.
