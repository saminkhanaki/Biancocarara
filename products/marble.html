Finishes

Surface options for marble.

Every finish below is available on request — tell us the application and we'll suggest the right one.

Polished

High gloss, reflects light, brings out full colour depth.

Honed

Matte, smooth surface — softer look, less slip in wet areas.

Brushed

Textured surface with a lightly worn, tactile finish.

Leathered

Subtle texture with a soft sheen, hides fingerprints well.

Colour Variants

Available marble tones.

Natural stone varies block to block — these represent typical colour ranges, not an exact match guarantee.

Bianco

Bright white ground with soft grey veining.

Grigio Venato

Cool grey base with pronounced linear veins.

Nero

Deep black ground with fine gold-grey veining.
