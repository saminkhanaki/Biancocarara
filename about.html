About Bianco Carrara

Two countries of stone, one point of contact.

"We buy the block ourselves, cut it ourselves, and stand behind every slab that leaves the yard."

Bianco Carrara works directly with quarry operators across Iran and Turkey — visiting the block face, grading for colour and vein consistency, and controlling the cut from raw stone to finished slab. That direct chain is what lets us quote honestly on lead time, and stand behind the stone that ships.

We work with architects, fabricators, contractors and developers on everything from single-slab feature walls to full building façades — supplying blocks, slabs and tiles in the finish and quantity a project actually needs.

Sourcing

Direct from the quarry, not a trading desk.

Origin is stated plainly on every product page — no stone is sold as something it isn't.

Iran

Our Iranian partners supply onyx graded for depth of colour and translucency, alongside fine marble and travertine blocks selected block by block at the face.

Turkey

Our Turkish partners supply travertine and granite from established quarry regions, cut to consistent tolerances for large-scale project orders.

How We Work

From brief to bill of lading.

Step 1

You send a brief

Stone type, finish, dimensions and quantity — even a rough idea is enough to start.

Step 2

We match blocks and quote

Available blocks or slabs, lead time, and FOB / CIF pricing, sent back within days.

Step 3

We cut, crate and ship

Export packing and documentation handled end-to-end, tracked to port of arrival.
